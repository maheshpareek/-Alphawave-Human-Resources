About US 

Alphawave Human Resources Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a firm conceptualized by Mr. Vyankatesh Sangamnerkar, a renowned professional having a work experience of more than 10 years in diversified profiles in the fields of Banking operations, Recruitments, Data mining, Lead generations, Telecom operations and Search engine optimization. Mr. Vyankatesh is a highly enthusiastic and motivated professional who is work oriented and team oriented person. With such a vast experience and having handled more than 2 Partnership Firms Mr. Vyankatesh decided to start with this venture to serve the best to his clients.

In the present Job scenario we know how important it is to find the right candidate and right job to ensure proper growth and development. A right job will not only provide you with desired professional growth but will also propel your career in the right direction. Likewise a right candidate will contribute substantially towards your successful business growth and development. So to bridge the gap we propose to be your helping hand ensuring not just the right fit but also providing you with the best consulting services hence shaping your overall success. As it is rightly said “Take care of your employees and they will take care of you”- we propose to do the exact same thing for you. We take care of all your employee related needs with our different services and in return provide you with a successful business experience.

With our different services like Recruitment services, Staffing solutions, Human Resources consulting we aim for 100% CLIENT SATISFACTION leaving no stones unturned to achieve the same. We have a team of highly efficient and dynamic professionals who work with full dedication to ensure reliable and on time services.
